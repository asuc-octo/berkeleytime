engineering.py¶

Engineering humanities and social sciences definition.

class mondaine.service.definition.engineering.EngineeringDefinition(definitions, *args, **kwargs)¶

Bases: mondaine.service.definition.constraint.ConstraintDefinition

A Definition for CoE Humanities and Social Sciences.

In order to satisfy this definition, course must satisfy all:

  1. Satisfy one of the following LSBreadthDefinitions:
    • Arts and Literature
    • Historical Studies
    • International Studies
    • Philosophy and Values
    • Social and Behavioral Science
Constraints:
  • Course must be at least three units
  • Course must not be in Engineering
  • Course number must not be 97, 98, 99, or above 196
Overrides:
  • BioE 100, CS C79, Engin 125, Engin 157AC and ME 191AC or 191K
satisfies(course)¶

Return True if the given Course object satisfies this definition.

haas.py¶

Definition for Hass breadth.

class mondaine.service.definition.haas.HaasBreadthDefinition(ls_definition, excluded_definitions=None, *args, **kwargs)¶

Bases: mondaine.service.definition.constraint.ConstraintDefinition

A definition for one Haas breadth requirement.

To satisfy this definition, course must satisfy all:

  1. Must satisfy the corresponding LS breadth definition
Constraints:
  1. Course must be at least three units
  2. Must not be a business course
  3. Econ 1/2/3/100{A|B}/101{A|B}, IAS 106/107 prohibited
  4. Course cannot satisfy R1A/R1B

Note: Haas breadths map 1-1 with L&S breadths.

satisfies(course)¶

Return whether the given course satisfies this requirement.

A general Definition mapping course.abbreviation : DepartmentDefinition.

In order to satisfy this definition, course must either:

  1. Satisfy at least one function in self.overrides

OR

2) Satisfy every function in self.constraints, and the course number must be allowed and not excluded in self.mapper

Constraints: - Course must be at least three units
