update(semester, year, abbreviation=None, course_number=None)¶

Update all sections for a particular course or all applicable courses.

Parameters:
  • semester – str The semester to update for (fall, spring, summer)
  • year – str The year to update for (e.g. ‘2017’)
  • abbreviation – str Course appreviation, e.g. COMPSCI. If abbreviation and course_number are both omitted, all courses will = have their sections updated.
  • course_number – str Course number, e.g. 61A
